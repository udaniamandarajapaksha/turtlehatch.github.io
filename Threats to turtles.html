Threats to turtles

Why Are Sea Turtles Endangered? 5 Major Threats

Threat Description
Pollution Polluted oceans affect turtle habitats and their food sources.
Climate Change Rising temperatures and sea levels disrupt nesting sites and migration patterns.
Predators Natural and introduced predators pose a threat to turtle eggs and hatchlings.
Coastal Development Loss of nesting beaches due to human activities and infrastructure development.
Illegal Trade Turtles are targeted for their meat, shells, and eggs, leading to population decline.
